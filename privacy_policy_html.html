Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This privacy policy describes how Weight Tracker - Simple Log ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information and protects your privacy.

Simple Summary: We don't collect, store, or see any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device and in your Apple Health app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your weight data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your Apple Health app if you choose to enable that feature.

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health sync:

  • We read existing weight data from your Health app to display in our app
  • We save new weight entries to your Health app when you log them
  • This data remains in your Apple Health app under your complete control
  • We cannot access this data without your explicit permission

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS data storage methods. We do not have servers, databases, or any way to access your data remotely.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone because we don't have access to it. Your weight information never leaves your device except when you choose to sync it with Apple Health.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app functions completely independently.

Data Export

The app includes a feature to export your data as a CSV file. This export is generated locally on your device and shared using standard iOS sharing methods. You control where this data goes.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
