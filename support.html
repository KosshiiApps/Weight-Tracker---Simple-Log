‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

How do I log my weight?
Go to the "Today" tab, tap the weight entry button, use the picker to select your weight, and tap "LOG WEIGHT".
How do I enable Apple Health sync?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Apple Health Sync ‚Üí Toggle it on. You'll be prompted to grant permissions to read and write weight data.
Can I change between pounds and kilograms?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Weight Unit and choose your preferred unit. Your existing data will be automatically converted.
How do I set a goal weight?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Goal Weight and use the picker to select your target. Your progress will be shown in the Progress tab.
Can I export my data?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data to save your weight log as a CSV file that you can share or backup.
Will Apple Health sync my old data?
The app only syncs data from when you enable Health sync forward. Old Health data won't automatically import to keep your tracking clean.
How do I clear all my data?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Clear All Data. This will remove all data from the app but won't affect your Apple Health data.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely! All data stays on your device and in your Apple Health app. We don't collect, store, or see any of your information.
